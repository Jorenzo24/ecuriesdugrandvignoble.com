Privacy policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

Data controller

The controller of the personal data collected on this website is:

Centre Équestre du Grand Vignoble
Le Grand Vignoble, 24140 Eyraud-Crempse-Maurens
Email: contact@ecuriesdugrandvignoble.com

Data we collect

We only collect the data you provide to us voluntarily through the contact form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • The content of your message

No data is collected automatically by our site (no audience-measurement cookies, no tracking pixels, no analytics tools).

Purpose of processing

The data we collect is processed solely to:

  • Reply to your enquiry
  • Arrange a booking (lesson, trail ride, course, livery)
  • Keep you informed about the progress of your request

Your data is never used for marketing purposes, never sold, and never passed on to third parties for prospecting.

Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent, given by voluntarily submitting the contact form (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), and on the legitimate interest of Centre Équestre du Grand Vignoble in managing its relationships with its contacts and clients (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Data recipients

The data from the form is passed on solely to:

  • The team at Centre Équestre du Grand Vignoble
  • Our email-sending provider (SMTP server), for the technical transmission of the message

No data is transferred outside the European Union, except for the third-party services detailed below.

Retention period

Messages received through the contact form are kept for as long as necessary to handle your request, and for no more than three years from the last contact, in line with the CNIL's recommendations.

Your rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Right of access to the data we hold
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (the 'right to be forgotten')
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@ecuriesdugrandvignoble.com. We will respond within a maximum of one month.

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you may lodge a complaint with the CNIL.

Cookies

This site does not place any cookies for audience measurement or advertising tracking.

Some embedded third-party services may, however, place their own cookies (see the following section).

Third-party services

Our site uses the following third-party services:

Google Fonts

The site's typefaces (Cormorant Garamond and Inter) are loaded from Google Fonts servers. When they load, your IP address may be transmitted to Google. More information: policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Maps

The map shown on some pages is embedded from Google Maps. When it loads, Google may collect browsing data (IP address, technical information). More information: policies.google.com/privacy.

Hosting

The site is hosted in Germany (European Union) with Hetzner Online GmbH, in compliance with the GDPR.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). The contact form transmits your data over a secure connection. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may change over time. Any changes will be published on this page along with the date of the update.
