Getting to the stables

Saint-Julien-de-Crempse, 24140
10 minutes north of Bergerac

The stables are about ten minutes north of Bergerac, near Saint-Julien-de-Crempse. They are easy to reach from Bergerac, Prigonrieux, Lembras, Creysse, La Force and Lamonzie-Saint-Martin, as well as from a little further afield such as Mussidan or Villamblard. Coming from Bergerac, sat-navs often stop at the manor house: carry on for about fifty metres — there is a large car park just after it, and the stables are right there.
