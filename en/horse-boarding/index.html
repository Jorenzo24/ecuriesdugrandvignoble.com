The estate

Natural grazing, roomy stables

In the heart of the Bergerac vineyards, the stables offer paddocks, natural grazing and spacious loose boxes. Everything is designed around the horse's wellbeing, for the long term. Calm, space, and the countryside for a neighbour.

The practicalities

Included in the livery, and down to you

What the livery covers, and what falls to you as the owner.

Included in the livery

  • Hay
  • Hard feed
  • Constant access to water
  • Bedding for the stables
  • Daily checks
  • Turnout (depending on the option)
  • Access to the facilities

Down to you

  • Farrier
  • Vet and equine dentist
  • Worming and specific care
  • Feed supplements
  • Personal rugs if needed
  • Horse insurance

Getting to the stables

Saint-Julien-de-Crempse, 24140
10 minutes north of Bergerac

Owners entrust their horses to us from Bergerac and well beyond — Prigonrieux, La Force, Mussidan, Villamblard, Vergt, as far as Montpon-Ménestérol and Sainte-Foy-la-Grande. Access is straightforward by trailer or lorry from the north of Bergerac, and the large car park in front of the stables gives you plenty of room to manoeuvre and unload in your own time. Please note: sat-navs often lead to the manor house, so keep going another fifty metres or so.
