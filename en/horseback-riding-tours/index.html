Horseback trail rides near Bergerac

An hour on horseback through the forests of the Bergerac countryside. Along quiet, narrow trails, at an easy walk and trot. For every age and every level.

The ride

An hour through the forest

The classic ride at the Écuries lasts an hour, winding through the forests of the Bergerac countryside. Along narrow trails where you travel in single file, at a gentle walk and trot. Longer rides are possible on request, depending on the group and the season.

Book a ride
The horses

Horses for everyone, at home on the trail

Our horses are chosen for their calm temperament and their experience out on the trails. They know the sounds of the forest, the tight passages and the unexpected encounters. Suited to every age and every level, from the complete beginner to the experienced rider. The trails are narrow by nature: you ride in single file, with no room to stray. A little extra safety, simply built into the route.

Good to know

Before you come

Everything you need to make the most of your ride.

Provided by the club

  • A horse matched to your profile
  • Riding hat (compulsory, provided)
  • A guide to lead the ride
  • Public liability insurance
  • A safety briefing before you set off

What to bring

  • Long trousers or jeans
  • Boots that cover the ankle, or short riding boots
  • A bottle of water for the longer rides
  • Clothing to suit the weather
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your start time

Find us

Getting to the stables

Saint-Julien-de-Crempse, 24140
10 minutes north of Bergerac

Riders come from all across the Bergerac area: Prigonrieux, Creysse and La Force close by, but also Mussidan, Villamblard, Vergt, Le Fleix and Sainte-Foy-la-Grande. Allow around ten minutes from the north of Bergerac. When you arrive, carry on past the manor house for about fifty metres: the car park and the starting point for the rides are just behind it.
