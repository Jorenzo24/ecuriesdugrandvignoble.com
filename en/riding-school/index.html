Riding school near Bergerac

Whether you ride for the occasional pleasure of it or you are training seriously, Écuries du Grand Vignoble welcomes you with open arms.

The riding school

To progress in your discipline

Group and private lessons, dressage and show jumping — choose the format that suits you.

Group lessons

Level-based group lessons, from beginner to galop 7. The momentum of a class to help you progress together.

Private lessons

One-to-one lessons with a tailor-made programme. Ideal for preparing an exam or a competition.

Dressage

Flatwork, suppling the horse, refining the aids. The foundation of any serious riding.

Show jumping

From your first cavalletti to competition courses. Introduction, refinement and competition preparation.

Progression

From first steps to competition

Working through galops 1 to 7, preparing for the French federation exams, heading out to competitions: every rider follows their own path at their own pace, with individual attention even in a group lesson.

Under the direction of Jacques Lambert, a professional rider, the stables have been producing competition riders for years. A shared drive to do well, in a genuinely friendly atmosphere.
